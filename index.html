What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple value calculated from a person's weight and height. It is used as a common indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

While BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not measure body fat directly and doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or fat distribution.
